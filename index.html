Quoc-Tuan Truong

I am a PhD student at SMU advised by Prof. Hady W. Lauw, and part of the Preferred.AI research group. My PhD focuses on multimodal learning, preference modeling and recommender systems. Previously, I was an undergraduate at VNU-UET, where I was advised by Prof. Xuan-Hieu Phan.

Currently, I also serve as Intel Student Ambassador for Artificial Intelligence. I had the fortune to be one of the ten Vietnamese recipients of the Honda Y-E-S Award in 2016.

I'm interested in how machines can learn useful representations and their applications of learning problems to the real-world.
