Quoc-Tuan Truong

I am a PhD student at Singapore Management University (SMU), advised by Prof. Hady W. Lauw, and part of Preferred.AI research group. I also serve as Intel Student Ambassador for Artificial Intelligence. Previously, I obtained my BSc degree in Computer Science from VNU Hanoi, where I was advised by Prof. Xuan-Hieu Phan.

I'm interested in developing better methods for information compression and retrieval problems. My PhD research spans multimodal representation learning, preference learning, and recommender systems.

I try to open source everything that I do. If you can't find code or a dataset related to our work, please contact me.
