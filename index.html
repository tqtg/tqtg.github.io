Quoc-Tuan Truong

I am a PhD student at Singapore Management University (SMU) advised by Prof. Hady W. Lauw, and part of Preferred.AI group. I also serve as Intel Student Ambassador for Artificial Intelligence. Previously, I was an undergraduate at VNU Hanoi, where I was advised by Prof. Xuan-Hieu Phan.

I'm interested in developing better models for information compression and retrieval problems. My PhD research spans multimodal learning, preference learning, and recommender systems.

I try to open source everything that I do. If you can't find code or a dataset related to our work, please drop me an email.
