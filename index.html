Quoc-Tuan Truong

I am a PhD student at Singapore Management University (SMU). My advisor is Prof. Hady W. Lauw. Previously, I obtained my BSc degree in Computer Science from Vietnam National University (VNU), Hanoi. During my PhD, I have received the SMU Presidential Doctoral Fellowships for two consecutive years 2019-2020. I was also fortunate to receive the Honda Y-E-S Award in Vietnam in 2016.

Research Interests:

  • Machine Learning Methods
  • Information Retrieval: Search and Recommendation
  • Learning from the Web and Human Behavioral Data

I try to open source everything that I do. If you can't find code or data related to our work, please contact me.
